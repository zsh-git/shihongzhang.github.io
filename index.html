About

Welcome to my academic homepage. I am interested in harmonic analysis, elliptic equations, and conformally invariant problems. I am especially interested in sharp constants, extremizers, and stability phenomena for functional inequalities.

Research

My recent work centers on sharp integral inequalities for conformally invariant extension operators and their adjoints, as well as related questions in stability theory and geometric analysis.

  • Sharp quantitative integral inequalities
  • Conformally invariant extension operators
  • Stability problems for functional and geometric inequalities
  • Hypergeometric function methods in analysis
